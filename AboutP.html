About the Story

The Catch is a dystopian psychological story that dives deep into themes of memory, silence, and resistance. Set in a world watched over by the system SIVIL, it follows Ottiline Quandt as she navigates secrets, grief, and growing rebellion within her fractured family and the society around her.

The story combines emotional tension with slow-burning mystery and character-driven plotlines. It challenges systems of control, explores the weight of what goes unspoken, and celebrates quiet acts of survival.

This project is written by Ca3kie and is being published in parts. Chapters 1 and 2 are available on Wattpad, while future chapters will be released here exclusively.

Meet Guild 7

A military woman. A pink-haired strategist. A boy who questions everything. A leader who never asked to lead. A silent girl who watches more than she speaks. A skeptic with wire-frame glasses and no faith in systems. A bruiser built for violence, quiet until he snaps. A soft-spoken thinker with eyes like radar. And one who hasn't said a single word — not even a name.
